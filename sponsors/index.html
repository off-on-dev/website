Sponsors

Sponsorship and Independence

Sponsors provide financial support and participate as community members, but do not control editorial direction, content priorities, or governance. This community belongs to its members.

Founding Sponsor

Dynatrace is the founding sponsor of OffOn. There is Dynatrace-related content in one dedicated category which you can choose to join, but the rest of the content is vendor-neutral and community-driven.

Ways to Support OffOn

There are multiple ways organisations can get involved with the OffOn community, beyond financial sponsorship.

Swag

Provide swag for community members and event attendees.

Software Licenses

Give community members access to tools and platforms for hands-on learning and challenges.

Distribution

Help spread the word through developer newsletters, event partnerships, or social channels.

Adventures and Challenges

Sponsor an adventure or challenge and help bring new open source learning content to the community.

How We Maintain Trust

  • Sponsors do not control editorial direction, content priorities, or community governance.
  • Promotional content is not permitted. Technical product mentions are welcome only when neutral and reproducible.
  • Our moderation policies and decision-making processes are documented and accessible.
  • Community input directly shapes how we evolve.
