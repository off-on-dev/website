our foundation

Our Mission

OffOn exists to sustain the people behind open source and create the maintainers of tomorrow.

We are a vendor-neutral, community-driven space built around one goal: supporting open source contributors and maintainers at every stage of their journey, from the first pull request to long-term stewardship.

Most platforms, foundations, and content formats today are designed with enterprises, legal teams, or marketing goals in mind. Far fewer are built for the individuals who actually keep open source alive.

As the ecosystem navigates new realities around regulation, digital sovereignty, and AI, OffOn gives the community a place to learn, share, mentor, and grow, from curious users to confident contributors, and from contributors to sustainable maintainers.

Our Vision

A future where open source is not only widely used, but actively sustained by a new generation of contributors and maintainers who are trained, supported, and connected, regardless of which company, country, or project they come from.

We believe a real community has to be open. Open in its tooling, open in its governance, and open in how knowledge is preserved and passed on. OffOn is our attempt to put that belief into practice.

Who It's For

  • Curious learners and developers looking for a practical, guided path into open source
  • Engineers, SREs, and practitioners who want to deepen their skills through real-world challenges
  • Contributors and maintainers who want to share knowledge and help grow the next generation
  • Advocates, OSPO members, and community builders who want to amplify their work and strengthen the wider ecosystem
  • Non-code contributors (writers, designers, translators, organisers) whose work is essential but often invisible
  • Organisations and sponsors who want to support open source learning and connect with the community on the community's terms

What We Stand For

  • Open and vendor-agnostic by default. Every challenge, tool, and piece of content is built around open source. Sponsors fund the work; they do not shape the mission.
  • Reproducible and action-oriented. Real-world scenarios, hands-on challenges, and practical knowledge you can apply, fork, and build on, not marketing in disguise.
  • Built for people, not pipelines. OffOn is not a sales channel and not a source of revenue. It exists to develop the maintainers of tomorrow.
  • Knowledge that lasts. Unlike chat tools and social platforms that lose context the moment a thread scrolls away, OffOn is designed to preserve, structure, and pass on what the community learns.
  • Respectful and inclusive. Constructive feedback, zero tolerance for harassment, and a space where every contribution, whether code, docs, design, or mentorship, is visible and valued.
our story

The Story Behind the Firefly

When we rebranded, we wanted the name and the mascot to feel true to this community. We talked through a lot of ideas and kept coming back to the same question: what mascot could reflect a space built on curiosity, contribution, and learning by doing?

At one point, someone said firefly, and it clicked. That is how Nyx came to life.

Nyx is more than a mascot. The firefly reflects what OffOn is about: people showing up for each other, sharing what they know, and building something brighter together. Each person brings their own spark, but together, we glow brighter.

That spark matters more than ever. AI can write code, but when it takes over all the work, how do people discover their passion or the creativity needed to design the best solutions? When curiosity fades, growth stops.

Understanding how things work still matters. When we combine our thinking, that is where the real magic happens. The power of community is not just about being part of something. It is an engine that fuels human connection, curiosity, and genuine love for a topic. AI cannot replicate that experience.
