    Community Guide

    Everything you need to get started, participate, and grow in the OffOn community.

    What is OffOn and who is it for?

    A practical, welcoming space for builders working with open standards and cloud-native technologies. OffOn focuses on reproducible, hands-on knowledge: tutorials, Q&A with accepted solutions, and recurring challenges, so members can learn quickly, share real-world implementations, and help each other succeed.

    What we stand for

    • Open, vendor-agnostic practices: OpenTelemetry, CNCF projects, and open source tooling
    • Reproducible, action-oriented content: code, configs, logs, screenshots
    • Respectful, inclusive collaboration: constructive feedback and zero tolerance for harassment

    Who it's for

    • Developers and engineers building cloud-native apps and services
    • SREs and DevOps practitioners focused on observability, reliability, and performance
    • Architects and tech leads designing telemetry pipelines and AI-assisted workflows
    • Community advocates and contributors who share tutorials, demos, and best practices
    • Curious learners seeking a guided, welcoming path into OpenTelemetry and cloud-native tools

    Vendor-agnostic Policy

    OffOn is vendor-agnostic. Technical product mentions are welcome when neutral and reproducible. Avoid promotional content. Marketing posts will be removed and repeated violations may lead to moderation actions. See Posting Guidelines (opens in new tab).
