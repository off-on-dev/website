Our Mission

OffOn is a vendor-neutral, community-driven space built around one goal: supporting open source contributors and maintainers at every stage.

As open source navigates new realities around regulation, digital sovereignty, and AI, OffOn gives the community a place to learn, share their work, and grow, from curious users to confident contributors, and from contributors to sustainable maintainers.

Who It's For

  • Curious learners and developers looking for a practical, guided path into open source
  • Engineers, SREs, and practitioners who want to deepen their skills through real-world challenges
  • Contributors and maintainers who want to share knowledge and help grow the next generation
  • Advocates and community builders who want to share their work and grow the open source ecosystem
  • Organisations and sponsors who want to support open source learning and connect with the community

What We Stand For

  • Open, vendor-agnostic practices: every challenge, tool, and content is built around open source
  • Reproducible, action-oriented content: real-world scenarios, hands-on challenges, and practical knowledge you can apply and build on
  • Respectful, inclusive collaboration: constructive feedback, zero tolerance for harassment, and a space where all contributions are visible and valued

How We Build Together

Learn by Doing

The best way to understand complex systems is to debug them. Every challenge is a real-world scenario with broken environments, misconfigured pipelines, and invisible failures.

Open Source First

We believe open source is the most powerful model for building technology that lasts. Free knowledge, shared tools, and no lock-in. That's the foundation everything here is built on.

Share What You Know

Post tutorials, design docs, and real-world case studies in Community Voices. Document what works, share what you have learned, and help others avoid the same hard lessons.

Grow Together

Mentor and be mentored. Ask questions in Q&A, share your experience, and help shape the next generation of contributors and maintainers.

People First

Open source work is often invisible, underfunded, and emotionally demanding. Whether contributing code, answering questions, or simply showing up to learn, your work is visible, valued, and supported here.

Vendor Agnostic

The best knowledge should be accessible to everyone. Technical product mentions are welcome when neutral and reproducible, because a more open ecosystem benefits the whole community.

Events & Meetups

List and host open source events for free. We're building the community home for tech meetup organisers, starting with no fees and no lock-in.

Built to Last

OffOn is here for the long term. We're committed to keeping this an inclusive, respectful space that contributors and maintainers can count on, year after year.

The Story Behind the Firefly

When we rebranded, we wanted the name and the mascot to say something true about this community. David put name ideas on the table, like OnOffOn.dev. In a team meeting, Katharina suggested offon.dev, and the whole team was in.

The mascot took longer. We tried AI and a few other approaches as a team. Sinduri became Kathi's sounding board as they talked through which creature fit a community like ours. In one of those conversations, Kathi heard "firefly", and she created the perfect logo.

The firefly is more than a mascot. Each of you is remarkable on your own. Together, we glow brighter.

Start Curious.Break Things.Learn Together.Glow Brighter.

Some species of fireflies synchronize their flashing. Thousands of individuals, each emitting their own signal, collectively producing something far more powerful than any single light source could alone.

That's OffOn. You bring your questions, your fixes, your ideas. We bring the challenges, the tooling, and the practitioners who care about the same problems you do.

Every spark starts with one person. Together, we illuminate the whole open source ecosystem.

Always On. Always Open. Always Learning.
